Controllers:

Controllers handle incoming HTTP requests and send responses back to the client. They use services to perform business logic and operations.

UserController
Methods for register, login, retrieve user profile, update profile, and delete account.
PostController
Methods for creating, retrieving, updating, and deleting posts.
CommentController
Methods for adding and deleting comments on posts.
ReactionController
Methods for adding and removing reactions to posts.
FollowController
Methods for following and unfollowing users.
